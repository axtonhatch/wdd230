Attractions

There are a lot of fun things to do in Provo. You can visit our collage campus BYU, which has a food court, bowling, and the Museum of art(MOA). There is a tasty virgin drink place called the wash that is very popular with the collage students. There is also a lot of cool hikes to do in rock canyon and the surrounding area.
